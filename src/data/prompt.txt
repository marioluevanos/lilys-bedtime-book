Four-year-old Lily has always had a vivid imagination; after all, life has been wonderful for the past four years. But then, everything changed when an unexpected new neighbor moved in, stealing the spotlight. As the new neighbor tries to fit in, Lily makes a surprising and eye-opening discovery one day. Is there more to the neighbor than meets the eye? When a sneaky scheme involving the neighbor threatens to take Lily's toys and disrupt her world, Lily and her trusty sidekick, Popcorn, must join forces to thwart the sinister plan. Will Lily and Popcorn show everyone who's in charge?
