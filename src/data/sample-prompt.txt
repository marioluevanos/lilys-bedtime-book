Four-year-old Lily has always had an overactive imagination; after all, life has been all peaches and cream for the past four years. And then, everything changed: an unexpected new neighbor arrived, stealing the show. As the new neighbor tries to fit in, one day, Lily makes a shocking, eye-opening discovery. Is there more to the neighbor than meets the eye? When a sneaky scheme involving the neighbor threatens to steal Lily's toys and change the world as We know it, Lily and Popcon will have to join forces to thwart the sinister plan. Will the Lily and Popcorn show everyone who's boss?
